Our Technologies

Electromagnetic Brain Pulse (EMBP)

EMBP is a personalized, non-pharmaceutical, and non-invasive neuromodulation treatment that alters nerve activity through the targeted delivery of stimulus to specific neurological sites in the body in order to help restore and optimize brain functionality.

Magnetic Stimulation for Viral Infection

Our technology for treating viral infections involves administering magnetic pulses cutaneously through a specially designed ergonomic coil to targeted nerve ganglia in order to deactivate the viral growth and proliferation.
